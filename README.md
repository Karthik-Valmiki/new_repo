# AI-Powered Parametric Insurance (PI) for Gig Workers

## 1. Problem Statement

Delivery partners in India (Zomato, Swiggy, Zepto, etc.) face **income volatility due to uncontrollable external disruptions** such as:

- Heavy rainfall
- Extreme heat
- High AQI
- Curfews / local restrictions
- Road blockages

These disruptions can reduce working hours, leading to **20–30% income loss per week**, with **no existing protection mechanism**.

### Core Problem

The problem is not just income loss — it is **income uncertainty**.

Workers cannot predict or recover from sudden disruptions, leading to financial instability.

---

## 2. Solution Overview

We propose a **parametric, AI-driven insurance platform** that:

- Detects disruptions using external data sources
- Validates whether the user is actively working
- Calculates income loss deterministically
- Issues **instant payouts without claims**
- Operates on a **weekly subscription model**

### Key Principle

> The system ensures workers do not experience sudden income drops during disruptions by compensating losses in real-time.

---

## 3. Target Persona

### Food Delivery Partner

- Works 8–12 hours/day
- Paid per delivery (variable income)
- Operates in fixed zones
- Depends entirely on mobile apps
- Needs predictable weekly earnings

### Persona Scenario

A delivery partner working during peak hours experiences sudden heavy rainfall:

- Deliveries drop significantly
- Earnings reduce to near zero
- No compensation from platform

With our system:

- Disruption is detected automatically
- Worker activity is validated
- Income loss is compensated instantly

---

## 4. Product Strategy

### Platform Choice

- **Mobile-first design (real-world usage)**
- **Web-based MVP (for hackathon implementation)**

### Rationale

- Workers rely on mobile (GPS, notifications, background tracking)
- Web MVP allows:
  - Faster development
  - Easier debugging
  - Stable demonstration
